Our Founder Jim McCann

The man who revolutionized the floral industry grew up with a bare patch of lawn outside his home occupied not by flowers, but by weeds. How ironic that Jim McCann would go on to take a single flower shop on Manhattan’s East Side and eventually turn it into a billion-dollar business, establishing one of the world’s leading gourmet food and floral gift providers.
